Structure for the trust

A Circle is a rotating savings group — the core unit of RAITON. Hover over any member below to see how each person participates in the rotation.

Hover a member

Hover over any name in the diagram to see their role in this rotation.

  • 01
    Admin creates the Circle

    Sets contribution amount, schedule, and rotation order. Invites members directly. No WhatsApp reminders needed.

  • 02
    Every member contributes each period

    Scheduled payments collected automatically. Funds held securely in Smart Escrow until payout day.

  • 03
    The designated member receives the payout

    One member receives the full pooled amount. Fixed. Guaranteed. Always on time. Never a surprise.

  • 04
    Rotation continues until everyone has received

    Every member receives exactly once. Your SFR score grows with each completed rotation.
